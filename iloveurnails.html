I Love Ur Nails

Custom Website for a Local Nail Salon – Designed for Client Engagement and Future Booking Integration

Project Overview

Designed and built a custom website for a local nail salon, focusing on creating an inviting online presence that reflects their brand identity. The site was built with a clean, modern aesthetic to enhance user experience and make it easy for clients to browse services and view the salon’s portfolio. I incorporated interactive galleries to showcase nail art, and structured the site for easy navigation. The core challenge was to create a strong front-end foundation that could be easily integrated with a future online booking system. This project allowed me to practice translating a client’s vision into a functional and visually appealing digital product, while also considering future scalability and user engagement.
